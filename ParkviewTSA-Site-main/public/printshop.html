Parkview Print Shop

Parkview Prints run by volunteers within the organization, is one of the numerous ways members can fundraise. In it, they design and create custom apparel, posters, banners, yard signs, and even items like keychains and cups. We offer products not only to Parkview, but for other schools, organizations and individuals as well.
