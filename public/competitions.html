CORE

The Chapter Officer Retreat for Excellence (CORE) program, located in The Kaplan Mitchell Retreat and Conference Center on September 7-9, 2024, is designed to provide Parkview TSA and other local chapter officers with an opportunity to develop interpersonal and leadership skills, gain necessary knowledge and abilities to carry out the duties of their individual offices, and more!

Tech Day

Located in Reaves Arena at the Georgia National FairGrounds in Perry, Georgia on October 7, Technology Day provides various competitions for students with a focus on engineering! For more information on Tech Day & events, visit this competition list! Parkview TSA proudly holds the title of 2023 Tech Day Superior Chapter, placing in the events below.

More Info

Fall Leadership Conference (FLC)

The annual Fall Leadership Conference, also known as FLC, is an excellent overnight trip from November 7th-10th at Jekyll Island, Georgia, that provides students with the opportunity to attend and teach leadership sessions to other GATSA chapters. Students can attend a variety of lessons, such as public speaking, and compete in various events, such as the Armada Boat Race & Sandcastle design.

More Info

State Leadership Conference

The annual State Leadership conference at Athens, Georgia, from March 12th-15th, gives students the opportunity to compete in 40+ events, ranging from coding to graphic design and leadership (see the TSA website for events list). As reigning 9-Time State Champions, winning 1st for the past 3 consecutive years, Parkview TSA strives to produce outstanding projects for each event.

Nationals

The TSA National conference is packed with competitive events and challenging activities that foster personal growth and leadership development. Each year TSA members are offered these and many more opportunities at the national conference. Placing in a multitude of events at the 2024 Orlando, Florida conference and attending the 2025 Nashville, Tennessee conference, Nationals opens up an infinite amount of opportunities for all participating TSA members.
