CORE

The Chapter Officer Retreat for Excellence (CORE) program, located in The Kaplan Mitchell Retreat and Conference Center on Sep. 6-8 2025, is designed to provide Parkview TSA and other local chapter officers with an opportunity to develop interpersonal and leadership skills, gain necessary knowledge and abilities to carry out the duties of their individual offices, and more!

Six Flags CTSO Day

Six Flags Over Georgia hosts an exciting CTSO (Career and Technical Student Organizations) Day where students from various organizations compete in creative and technical events. This event combines fun at the theme park with competitive spirit, allowing students to showcase their skills in design, presentation, and promotional materials while enjoying the thrill of Six Flags!

Fall Leadership Conference (FLC)

The annual Fall Leadership Conference, also known as FLC, is an excellent overnight trip from November 14th-16th at Jekyll Island, Georgia, that provides students with the opportunity to attend and teach leadership sessions to other GATSA chapters. Students can attend a variety of lessons, such as public speaking, and compete in various events, such as the Armada Boat Race & Sandcastle design.

Learn More

State Leadership Conference

The annual State Leadership conference at Athens, Georgia, from March 12th-15th, gives students the opportunity to compete in 40+ events, ranging from coding to graphic design and leadership (see the TSA website for events list). As reigning 9-Time State Champions, winning 1st for the past 3 consecutive years, Parkview TSA strives to produce outstanding projects for each event.
