Fundraising

SuccessFund

Year Long

To fundraise for our chapter, Parkview TSA uses SuccessFund for online payments. It allows our competitive members to donate online with ease.

STEM Academy

09/11/25 - 10/30/25

STEM Academy serves as Parkview TSA's best events. Here, we bring in 50 kids from our local elementary school, Camp Creek, and create awesome STEM Projects with them once a week for 6 weeks. This year's theme was Carnival!

Punch Cards

Year Long

Parkview TSA wants to make fundraising more exciting. So, we created Punch Cards. Every time a member donates $5, they get a funny picture of an officer.

Parkview Day

11/1/25

Parkview Day is a special day where everyone fundraises for their organization. With the help of our hard-working members, TSA sold sweatshirts, hoodies, and more.

Parkview Prints

Year Long

Parkview TSA has their very own printshop, where they design everything from banners to T-Shirts. To fundraise for their competitions, members can opt in to volunteer.
