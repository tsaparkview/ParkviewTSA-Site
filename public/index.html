Who is Parkview TSA?

The Technology Student Association (TSA), a prominent national organization, focuses on cultivating STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics) skills among middle and high school students. Parkview TSA provides a platform for students to engage in a wide variety of competitions at both state and national levels, allowing them to apply and enhance their technical and leadership abilities. At Parkview High School, TSA is known for its excellence and dedication, boasting over 125 active members. We are proud to be one of the largest and most successful TSA chapters in the state. In addition, Parkview celebrates being 9-TIME STATE CHAMPIONS this year, winning top 10 in over 25 competitions in the 2023-2024 school year. Hail Parkview!

STEM Academy

STEM Academy is an after-school program that Parkview TSA hosts for students in grades 3-5 at Camp Creek Elementary to learn about the basics of engineering while creating daily projects. Members may volunteer for points and fun!

VEX Robotics

VEX Robotics challenges students to build, program, and drive robots in an expansive competition that covers the entire school year. They work along side TSA and even compete in state and national events!
