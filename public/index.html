Who are We?

TSA, or the Technology Student Association, is a national organization centered around STEM for middle school and high school students. We compete in state-wide and national events while learning and applying leadership skills in everything we do. Parkview High School is considered one of the best TSA chapters in the state. With over 150+ members, TSA stands as one of the biggest clubs at Parkview High School.

STEM Academy

STEM Academy is an after-school program that Parkview TSA hosts for students at Camp Creek (3rd-5th grade) to learn about the basics of engineering while creating daily projects.

VEX Robotics

VEX Robotics challenges students to build, program, and drive robots in an expansive competition that covers the entire school year. They work along side TSA and even compete in state and national events!
