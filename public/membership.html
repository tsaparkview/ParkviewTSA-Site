Membership Recruitment & Recognition

End of Year Banquet

05/2/25

As the 24-25 school year came to a close, Parkview TSA partnered with HOSA and DECA to host an End of the Year Banquet. At this event, we honored our members with their senior cords and announced new officer positions for the next school year.

Open House

07/31/25

To reel in more members into our organization, Parkview TSA hosted a table during open house to introduce all Parkview students to everything TSA.

Interest Meeting

08/12/25

Our first annual interest meeting was held in the 2nd week of school. We had an excellent turnout with over 200 students present.

Freshmen Advisement Recruitment

08/08/25 & 08/11/25

During these few days, our Parkview TSA officers visited every freshman advisement period and promoted our chapter, ultimately leading us to have 200+ active members.

Recruitment Week

08/06/25 - 08/07/25

On both of these days, our officers promoted our chapter by setting up a table with a spinning wheel to invite members to our interest meeting. Additionally, we recruited a list of people for a raffle for the meeting.

Member of the Month

Monthly

Every month, we have a special member who has committed to doing their all for the chapter. To celebrate them, we give them a comfortable Parkview TSA sweater they can wear whenever and wherever they please!

Social Media

This year, we reached a milestone of 1000 followers on our Instagram! Our chapter is eternally grateful for every one of them.

Senior Cords

05/02/25

To show our appreciation to our diligent seniors, Parkview TSA gives them 3 cords (Red, White, and Blue) that they can wear with them as they collect their diploma during graduation.

TSA Week

10/06/25 - 10/10/25

Parkview TSA makes it a tradition to participate in Georgia TSA's TSA Week to promote our chapter and the entire CTSO.

300 Members Milestone

2025-2026 School Year

This year, Parkview TSA reached an incredible milestone of 300 members (all not shown), making it one of the largest and most active TSA chapters in Georgia. This achievement reflects our commitment to providing opportunities for students interested in technology, leadership, and innovation.
