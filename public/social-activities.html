We organized a Halloween Social in the month of October with other CTSOs, including fun activities like pumpkin carving, a costume contest, and a relay. Some of the other activities were pumpkin decorating and face painting. We all made it more special by having pizza and cookies together.

For our winter social we had fun games such as gingerbread decoration competition, snowplow, snowball toss, and the candy cane game. We also had an ugly sweater contest and white elephant and gave out goody bags as member appreciation.
